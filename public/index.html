CityDog Tracker

Live tracking of where the ferries are on the Brisbane River. Can't spot Bluey or Bingo? They've probably gone for a nap, and will be back soon!

A little more info

Brisbane's main ferries are known as the CityCats and the little inner city ones are known as the KittyCats. With everyone's favourite show Bluey being set primarily in Brisbane, and to celebrate Bluey's World opening, two of our CityCats have been transformed into CityDogs. With that, I thought to whip together a quick live map of our ferries to make it easier to find where the CityDogs are at.

Sometimes the CityDogs named Bluey and Bingo aren't running an active route or live tracking is temporarily unavailable, so they won't appear on the map.

This map is made possible by Translink's GTFS-RT Feeds and Leaflet. A little project by JimmyD
